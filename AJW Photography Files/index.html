M E

Hey you! This is a website about my work. You can find photos and videos that I have captured over the years. I got my first camera when I was 9, and soon after took a workshop in photography. I have interests in political science; but film is my passion. Influences for my work flow from directors like Wes Anderson and Stanley Rubrich and Youtubers like Casey Neistat and Jacksgap. Everytime I look through a camera, I do so with the desire to create something that nobody has ever seen before. So. check. out. this. site.
